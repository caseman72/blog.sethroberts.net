]]> By: Jill http://blog.sethroberts.net/2012/01/18/sopa-strike/#comment-946564 Wed, 18 Jan 2012 19:31:25 +0000 http://blog.sethroberts.net/?p=5470#comment-946564 I don’t have anything to say about SOPA, but your statement, “If those who benefit from the status quo usually win, problems stack up unsolved,” suggests that the status quo is usually problematic and change is usually beneficial for solving those problems. I’d think you should be a little more circumspect. If there is a problem caused by the status quo, change would be necessary to solve it, but that doesn’t mean any change that benefits someone is going to solve the problem (it can and often does make the problem worse). Also, just because someone benefits from change doesn’t automatically mean there’s a problem with the status quo.

Of course, I do understand that your focus is innovation.
