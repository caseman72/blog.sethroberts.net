]]> By: Alex Chernavsky http://blog.sethroberts.net/2012/03/30/fermented-foods-improve-irritable-bowel-syndrome/#comment-961576 Sat, 31 Mar 2012 11:31:35 +0000 http://blog.sethroberts.net/?p=6199#comment-961576 My wife suffers from chronic digestive problems, probably IBS (other possible diagnoses have been ruled-out by tests). She finds significant relief from drinking kombucha. Before trying kombucha, she tried various drugs — to no effect. She also tried soy yogurt and various probiotics sold at health food stores, but none of those treatments had any effect.

Drinking kombucha is not a cure. She drinks eight or ten ounces every day, but she still has flare-ups of her symptoms. Nevertheless, she’s much better now than before.
