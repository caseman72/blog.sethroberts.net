If I have a very dark blue pair of pants, I’ll tend to see them as black if I wear them with a white shirt. But, if I lay out the dark blue pants against a black shirt, I’ll see it as blue. Same, even with the color black itself. When I lay out blue-black against red-black against green-black, I can see the differences against each other.
