Doctors, naturopaths, herbalists, acupuncturist, holistic MD’s, biofeedback, and medications did not help much or for long. My son follows a modified version of my diet and has a migraine less than every month. My best diet consists of meat, salt, butter, (Dr. Mercola at http://articles.mercola.com/sites/articles/archive/2010/12/07/why-is-butter-better.aspx) has great info regarding the benefits of butter!), raw milk, egg yolk, cabbage, kale, lettuce, carrots, potatoes, apples, pears, and mango.
David Buchholz, MD (Johns Hopkins neurologist) wrote a book, Heal Your Headache: The 123 Program, about the strong connection between headaches and diet and has a good explanation for the difficulty in determining headache triggers. And this sight is great for info on the potentially problematic chemicals in foods http://failsafediet.wordpress.com/.
