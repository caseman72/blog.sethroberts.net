More or less, you view the world, come up with a mental model, decide what to do, and then act. You repeat the cycle over and over. If you can do these cycles quicker than your opponent, you get more info about the battlefield quicker, giving you an advantage. Self-experimenters seem to go through their OODA loop or decision cycle quicker than academic experimenters.
