It is possible that my experience is a transient phenomenon, but time will tell and so far it’s holding.

I grew up on a dryland wheat farm. Innovation was a part of daily life. Dad and Uncles were always looking for a better way to do things. A friend of Dad’s, needing a bigger tractor, took the front wheels off two tractors, hooked the front of one to the reinforced hitch of the other, making a pivot point. On the farm, we were always fixing things, trying new ways. Technology was a significant part of daily life.
