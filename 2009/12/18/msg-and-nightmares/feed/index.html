There is a definite link between coffee, glutamates and nightmares/anxiety. I am typing this at 3:49 am after a terrible nightmare and I am afraid to go back to sleep. I do have schizoaffective bipolar disorder but the lithium was making the “vivid dreams” tolerable. I also take Ritalin for excessive daytime sleepiness due to severe sleep apnea. No more for me until I get fully tapered off the valium. Oh yeah, and for some reason eating chicken parmesan made me anxious until I read that cooked tomatoes are full of natural glutamates.
