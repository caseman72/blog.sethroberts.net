So: 14 cups of milk in double boiler to 190F, water bath to drop to 130F, add about a cup of cold yogurt to the hot milk, mix, pour into containers, into the 130F bath. 3 hrs later into the fridge. I guess you could say it’s mild; my family says they like that. The bath often is as low a 110 when I start. I use a picnic cooler for the hot water bath.
