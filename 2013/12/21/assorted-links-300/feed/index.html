The aspirin at night study didn’t find that “Patients who took blood pressure medication at night were about a third as likely to have a heart attack or stroke as morning medication takers”. That was some other study. This aspirin study found no effect on blood pressure of when the aspirin was taken.

It also didn’t find that taking aspirin in the evening vs morning reduced stroke or heart attack rates.
