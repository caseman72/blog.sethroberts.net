Too-long clotting time certainly is dangerous — the danger is of a stroke. I eat ground flaxseed because it has substantial easy to notice benefits (e.g., pink gums instead of reddish gums). If I were in your position, I would start with a tiny dose and increase it. At the first sign of bruising, I would reduce the dose to a dose that doesn’t cause bruising. I would also try to find out if my clotting time (without flaxseeds) was unusually long.
