It’s what will happen, though. If memristors don’t succeed in displacing flash that way, that might be the end of memristors. If they do succeed, we might eventually see much better uses that depend on fundamentally different models of computation. The first such uses will probably be in specialized real-time image or signal processing, and others only decades later.
