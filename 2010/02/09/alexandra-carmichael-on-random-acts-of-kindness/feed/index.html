Seth, you’ve written that much of your self-experimentation has pointed to the wisdom of returning to our more ancient lifestyles: morning sunlight, fewer ditto foods, stand more, etc. I speculate this may be another example of that: before the invention of money or the bartering of goods, the main currency of human societies was probably reciprocity. By that logic it wouldn’t be surprising that helping others raises your own self-worth, as in a sense you are accumulating “assets.”
